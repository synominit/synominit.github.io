About Me

The quote "A jack of all trades is a master of none, but oftentimes better than a master of one" comes to mind when explaining my skillsets and IT career. I love Automation, I am always looking for opportunities to turn slow, repetitive, error prone tasks into fast, consistent and reliable processes.

My experiences include a wide range of IT roles, from Support, Software Development, to Networking and System Administration.

I have experience in maintaining CI/CD pipelines on hybrid on-prem vSphere, AWS cloud infrastrucures in code with Terraform, Ansible, Jenkins. I can also develop custom desktop, web applications and automate almost any task that requires a computer.

I am currently a free lance developer that is available to work on projects or get hired on as a full time DevOps Engineer.
